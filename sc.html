Responsibilities and Experience

The Student Council at Vidyalankar Institute of Technology strives to brainstorm and come up with creative and unique ideas for events that are held in our college. Presenting events is not the only objective of the Student Council. The multi-talented members of the Council work with faculty, administration as well as people from outside their college campus, and that helps them to develop their personality as well as improve their relations with the student body of other colleges. We, the Student Council members, work towards initiating, implementing and actualising our vision, not just from the Technical aspect, but from Cultural, Literary and Sports aspect too, and more!

The first year as Publicity Extended, Second with maximum hardwork as Creative and Documentation Core and leading as Creative Head in the third year. Through the journey, Student Council became a family and the bond resulted in some phenomenal events.
